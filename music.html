Catikia's Music

Catikia played trombone in school in Concert, Symphonic, Marching and Jazz Bands, and earned 1st and 2nd ratings in several solo and ensemble festivals. She sang in choir in high school as well as church, where she also sang a duet. She played flute in ensemble with friends and has dabbled in several other instruments.

In 2009 she began teaching music privately while working in accounting, and she joined Maricopa Music Circle in May of 2012. Currently she sings Karaoke while playing piano & guitar on Twitch.

Solo's

Catikia has played several solo pieces on piano and guitar. Her favorite genre's to sing are country, rock, and pop.













Groups
